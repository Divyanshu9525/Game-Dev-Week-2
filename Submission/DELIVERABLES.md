
## Submission

All submissions go through **CSOC portal**. A detailed workflow will be given in the classroom.

1. Join the classroom via the CSoC website — the link is available once you're registered.
2. Your submission status is tracked and visible on the website.
3. Make two different scenes for the Exercise 2 and 3. 
4. Make sure your repo includes:
    - [ ]  A `README.md` with a short description.
    - [ ]  A `demo.md` that contains a demo video of your work.
    - [ ]  A `details.txt` that contains your personal details.
    - [ ]  Your Blender files (.blend) and exported FBX files.
    - [ ]  Your Unity project with the animation systems implemented.
5. **Do not** include the `Library/` or `Temp/` folders — add them to your `.gitignore`.
